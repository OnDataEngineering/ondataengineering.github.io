The Mid Week News 05/06/2019 edit  

2019-06-05 Peter

If you’ve been following us on twitter (@OnDataEng) then you’ll have seen all this news already.

If not - here’s your new weekly news…

Technology updates (details are on the relevant technology pages):

  • Qubole Data Service R56 is out for AWS, Azure and Oracle
  • Hortonworks (Cloudera) Data Steward Studio 1.5 is out
  • Apache Storm has a big 2.0 release out
